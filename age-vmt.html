Vehicle Miles Traveled (VMT) by Age

This page shows median cumulative vehicle miles traveled (VMT) by vehicle age. Each chart displays the 50th quantile (median) of total mileage for a given combination of powertrain and vehicle type as vehicles age over time. The “Data” tab shows the raw data, which also includes the 25th and 75th quantiles. Lines that are less smooth are the result of less data for that particular combination of powertrain and vehicle type. See the about page for more details.
